Aspen, Colorado

Music is the shorthand of emotion

Welcome to Aspen, all ye Road Scholars! We're going to have a great time together - we can't wait to show you this magical place which has been made yet more magical by the incredible music you're about to hear at the Aspen Music Festival.

Aspen is delightfully small (see below Google map) - you can walk everywhere. Get out those phones and feel free to wander! Great restaurants abound for every taste and budgetary approach.

The big red pin is Wagner Park which is right in the middle of town.

Things to take into account here in Aspen

  • Altitude - we're delightfully high here in Aspen, around 8,000 feet
  • We're that much closer to the sun, so have suncreen, hat, and lip balm handy
  • Drink lots of water - we've got water for you on the bus
  • Our schedule is action packed, so when opportunity offers take a nap!
